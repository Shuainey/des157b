Proposal

   My topic is broad. I basically want to split the seal into multiple sections representing the different aspects of Davis. Each section will consist of a "mini-game" relating to the topic at hand. For example, the map part of the seal will have a "Geo-guesser" type mini-game to it where the user is provided a picture of a known place in UC Davis and is prompted to put a pin on where the user think the place is located at. This is merely one of many mini-games that my partner, Brian Ton, and I are planning to implement. As for the target audience, I think it is very broad as the age-friendly games will attract children while the informative and engaging interface will catch the audults attentions as well. Although I feel like this project is on the ambitious side, I do believe that since I do have a partner who is passionate and willing to put in the hours, like myself, it will defintatly be possible.
